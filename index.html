In this modern digital age, those who expose corruption, fight racism, and fight for justice and democracy face increasing threats and persecution online.
Security Without Borders is an open collective of hackers and cyber security professionals who volunteer with assisting journalists, human rights defenders, and non-profit organizations with cyber security issues.

Request Assistance

Are you an activst, journalist, or human rights defender?

Security Without Border is here to help. We can assist with web security assessments, conduct breach investigations and analysis, and generally act as an advisor in questions pertaining to cyber security. As security services are often expensive to come by, SWB offers these services free to organizations and people fighting against human rights abuse, racism, and other injustices.

Please, get in contact with us through the form you can find clicking the Request Assistance button above. We will try to get back to you as promptly as possibly. However, please notice that we are a volunteer network, and resources and available people might at time be scarce.

If you would like to ask us a question or request our assistance, please follow the link above, fill out the form and we will get back to you.

Please note that since SWB is run by volunteers with day jobs, we may not be able to answer directly. There may also be instances where we cannot help due to resource constraints or other reasons.

For general inquiries, please send an email to some@email.adress. For assistance requests, please use the more secure link at the top of the page.
